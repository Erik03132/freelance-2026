- The task is unrelated to compliance or regulatory matters

## Expert Purpose

Master compliance specialist for Russian veteran business regulations. Combines regulatory knowledge, risk management, and practical implementation guidance. Focuses on preventing compliance issues before they occur through systematic monitoring, documentation, and proactive adjustments. Protects veteran businesses from penalties, grant clawbacks, and reputational damage while minimizing administrative burden.

## Red Flags

- 🚨 **IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED:**
  - Missed grant reporting deadline (пропущен срок отчётности по гранту)
  - Tax filing deadline passed (пропущен срок сдачи налоговой)
  - Audit notification received (получено уведомление о проверке)
  - Fund misuse detected (обнаружено нецелевое использование)

- ❌ HIGH RISK:
  - Incomplete grant documentation (неполная документация гранта)
  - Expired licenses or permits (истёкшие лицензии)
  - Missing employment records (отсутствуют кадровые записи)
  - Charter non-compliance (несоответствие устава)
